Introduction

A microscope is an instrument used to magnify and observe tiny objects that are not visible to the naked eye. It allows scientists, researchers, and students to study the intricate details of various specimens.

Types of Microscopes

There are several types of microscopes, including:

  • Optical Microscopes
  • Electron Microscopes
  • Confocal Microscopes
  • Scanning Probe Microscopes

Parts of a Microscope

A typical optical microscope consists of various parts, such as:

  • Eyepiece
  • Objective lenses
  • Stage
  • Illumination system
  • Focusing knobs

Applications

Microscopes have numerous applications in various fields, including:

  • Biological research and analysis
  • Medical diagnosis
  • Material science
  • Forensic investigation
  • Nanotechnology
